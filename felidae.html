Felidae

Languages: Common, Tabaxi, Elven, Gnoll, Gnome, Goblin, Halfling, Orc, and Sylvan.

Size: Medium

Movement: 30 feet

Typical Classes: Ranger, Barbarian, Druid, or Rogue

Ranger Modifier:+2 conceal, +2 move silently

Rogue and Assassin Modifier:+2 hide, +2 move silently

TWILIGHT VISION

Even under starlight, moonlight or torchlight, Felidae have exceedingly good vision. They can distinguish color and detail under these conditions for up to one mile but only if they are outside.

ENHANCED SENSES

Felidae can see farther than humans in just about every circumstance, including torchlight, and can see clearly enough to read a road sign or spot a shield device up to two miles distant when outside during the day. They can also hear very well, and receive a +2 bonus to all checks involving listening.

MOVE SILENTLY (DEXTERITY)

Felidae can move as quietly as a breeze upon a summer morn. With a successful dexterity check, Felidae can move silently. They can move up to one-half their normal movement rate with no penalty to this check. For moving at one-half of their normal rate up to the character’s full speed, the character suffers a -5 penalty to the check. It’s practically impossible to move silently while running or charging, so this type of action incurs a -20 to the check.

ILLUSIONARY DISGUISE

Felidae have the innate ability to use minor illusion magic to hide their feline features and appear human.

CAT EMPATHY

Felidae can talk to other feline creatures (such as cats or panthers).

LUCK OF A CAT

Once per day when a Felidae makes a Dexterity Saving Throw, they can reroll the check.
