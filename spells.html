Acid Arrow

Level 2 Wizard SV none
CT 1 SR yes
Range 450 ft. Comp V, S, M
Duration 1 rd. + 1 rd./2lvl School Conjuration

A magical arrow of acid springs from the caster’s hand and speeds toward a single target. The caster must succeed with a ranged touch attack to hit the target. The acid arrow deals 2d4 points of acid damage if it hits. For every two caster levels the acid, unless somehow neutralized, lasts an additional round and deals another 2d4 points each round (2 rounds at 3rd to 4th level; 3 rounds at 5th to 6th level, etc.).

Material component The material component for this spell is a drop of acid or some type of corrosive substance.
