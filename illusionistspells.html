Illusionist Spell List

Illusionist Spells

0th-Level Illusionist Spells (Cantrips)

  • Arcane Mark: Inscribes a personal rune (visible or invisible).
  • Dancing Lights: Creates figment torches or other lights.
  • Detect Illusion: Detects illusions along 50 ft. path.
  • Dragon Mark: Creates dragon sounds on other side of door.
  • First Aid Bandages bleeding wounds.
  • Influence Temporary charm.
  • Light Object shines like a torch.
  • Magical Aura: Grants object false magic aura.
  • Message: Short, whispered communication at a distance.
  • Mending: Makes minor repairs on an object.
  • Prestidigitation: Performs minor tricks or tasks.

1st-Level Illusionist Spells

  • Change Self: Changes the caster’s appearance.
  • Charm Person: Makes one person the caster’s friend.
  • Color Spray: Knocks unconscious, blinds, or stuns 1d4+1 creature per level of caster.
  • Darkness*: 20 ft. radius of supernatural darkness.
  • Daze: Creature loses next action.
  • Dragon Armor: Phantom armor grants +1 AC to those touched.
  • Dragon Image: 1d6 damage.
  • Erase: Mundane or magical writing vanishes.
  • Faerie's Glamour: Makes another appear as illusionist.
  • Head Fog: Victim suffers -4 to hit for 1d4+1 rds.
  • Hypnotism: Fascinates 2d4 HD of creatures.
  • Illusionary Hounds: Two hounds distract opponents.
  • Minor Dark Chaos: Whiplike tendrils cause 3 hp damage, last 2 rds.
  • Obscuring Mist: Fog surrounds the caster.
  • Read Magic: Read scrolls and spellbooks.
  • See Invisibility: Reveals invisible creatures or objects.
  • Silent Image: Creates minor illusion of your design.
  • Undetectable Aura: Masks magic item’s aura.
  • Ventriloquism: Throws sound or voice.
  • Ward's Temporary Strength: Strength +1 for 1 minute/level.

2nd-Level Illusionist Spells

  • Alter Self: As change self, plus more drastic changes.
  • Angelic Image: Delays undead.
  • Blur: +2 bonus to Armor Class against attacks.
  • Cure Light Wounds: Cures 1d8 damage.
  • Dark Chaos: Whiplike tendrils cause 6 hp damage, last 2 rds.
  • Detect Magic: Detects magical aura & its strength along 50 ft. path.
  • Detect Thoughts: Allows “listening” to surface thoughts.
  • Dragon Bite: Bite like dragon, 2d6 damage.
  • Eyes Of Fire: Fiery eyes provide light and scare enemies.
  • False Trap: Makes item seem trapped.
  • Fog Cloud: Fog obscures vision.
  • Hypnotic Pattern: Fascinates 2d4+1 HD/level of creatures.
  • Invisibility: Subject is invisible until it attacks.
  • Magic Mouth: Speaks once when triggered.
  • Minor Image: Image, plus some sounds and smells.
  • Mirror Image: Creates 1d4+1 decoy duplicates of the caster.
  • Misdirection: Misleads divinations for one creature or object.
  • Pyrotechnics: Turns fire into blinding light or choking smoke.
  • Remove Blindness/Deafness*: Makes subject blind or deaf.
  • Ward's Temporary Invisibility: Make objects invisible for 1 minute/lvl.

3rd-Level Illusionist Spells

  • Blink: Caster moves in/out of ethereal plane and is difficult to hit.
  • Continual Flame: Makes a permanent, heatless torch.
  • Dispel Magic: Cancels magical spells and effects.
  • Dispel Illusion: Cancels illusion spells and effects.
  • Displaced Image: Creates copy of creature to draw attacks.
  • Doubled Treasure: Viewer believes treasure hoard is doubled.
  • Dragon Mount: Can carry 4 creatures; fly, swim, or walk 60 ft. movement.
  • Explosive Runes: Deals 6d6 damage when read.
  • Hallucinatory Terrain: Makes one type of terrain appear like another.
  • Hold Person: Holds one person helpless; 1 round/level.
  • Illusionary Help: Summons situation aid.
  • Illusory Script: Only intended reader can decipher.
  • Invisibility Sphere: Makes everyone within 10 ft. invisible.
  • Major Image: As silent image, plus sound, smell & thermal effects.
  • Nondetection: Hides subject from divination, scrying.
  • Rope Trick: Up to eight creatures hide in extradimensional space.
  • Scare: Panics one creature up to 5 HD.
  • Secret Page: Changes one page to hide its real content.
  • Suggestion: Compels subject to follow stated course of action.
  • Tongues: Speak any language.

4th-Level Illusionist Spells

  • Charm Person: Makes monster believe it is the caster’s ally.
  • Confusion: Makes subjects behave randomly for 1 rd/level.
  • Cure Serious Wounds: Cures 3d8 damage.
  • Dragon Scales: +4 AC to caster and allies within 15 ft. radius.
  • Emotion: Arouses strong emotion in subject.
  • Fear: Subject flees for 1 round/level.
  • Idol Of Death: Clay figure absorbs 3 hp melee damage.
  • Illusory Wall: Surface looks real but anything can pass through.
  • Improved Invisibility: As invisibility but can attack & stay invisible.
  • Major Dark Chaos: Whiplike tendrils cause 10 hp damage, lasts 2 rds.
  • Minor Creation: Creates one cloth or wood object.
  • Mirage Arcana: As hallucinatory terrain, plus structures.
  • Phantasmal Killer: Illusion kills subject or deals 3d6 damage.
  • Rainbow Pattern: Prevents 24 HD of creatures from attacking or moving away.
  • Secure Shelter: Creates sturdy, furnished cottage.
  • Seeming: Changes appearance of one person/two levels.
  • Shadow Conjuration: Shadow monsters attack foes.
  • Solid Fog: Blocks vision and slows movement.
  • Treasure Hoard: Victims grab illusionary treasure and run.
  • Ward's Illusionary Portal: Dimensional portal for items to illusionist’s home.

5th-Level Illusionist Spells

  • Dragon Breath: Breath like dragon for 5d6 damage.
  • Dragon Shadow: Shadow grants +2 AC and bites for 3d6 damage.
  • Dream: Sends message to anyone sleeping.
  • Faithful Hound: Phantom dog can guard, attack.
  • False Vision: Fools scrying with an illusion.
  • Greater Shadow Conjuration: Strong shadow monsters attack foes.
  • Guards And Wards: Array of magic effects protect area.
  • Hold Monster: As hold person, but any creature.
  • Humanoid Finding: Locates humanoid over far distance.
  • Major Creation: As minor creation, plus stone and metal.
  • Mass Suggestion: As suggestion, plus one/ level subjects.
  • Mirror Wall: Impenetrable, mirrored wall.
  • Neutralize Poison: Detoxifies venom in or on subject.
  • Nightmare: Sends vision dealing 1d10 damage, fatigue.
  • Persistent Image: As major image, but no concentration required.
  • Project Image: Illusory double can talk and cast spells.
  • Secret Chest: Hides magic chest for caster to retrieve when needed.
  • Shadow Evocation: Mimics certain wizard spells.
  • True Seeing: See all things as they really are.
  • Ward's Extended Invisibility: Make objects invisible for 1 day/lvl.

6th-Level Illusionist Spells

  • Anti-Illusion Shield: Breath like dragon for 5d6 damage.
  • Cloak Of Dark Chaos: Shadow grants +2 AC and bites for 3d6 damage.
  • Cure Critical Wounds: Sends message to anyone sleeping.
  • Feeble Mind: Phantom dog can guard, attack.
  • Geas: Fools scrying with an illusion.
  • Greater Shadow Evocation: Strong shadow monsters attack foes.
  • Illusionary Lions: Array of magic effects protect area.
  • Mislead: As hold person, but any creature.
  • Permanent Image: Locates humanoid over far distance.
  • Programmed Image: As minor creation, plus stone and metal.
  • Shades: As suggestion, plus one/ level subjects.
  • Veil: Impenetrable, mirrored wall.

7th-Level Illusionist Spells

  • Awe: Enemies suffer loss of 1 in each attribute, loss of primes
  • Insanity: Subject suffers continuous confusion.
  • Mass Invisibility: As invisibility, but affects all in range.
  • Maze: Traps subject in extradimensional maze.
  • Power Word Stun: Creatures with up to 120 hp are stunned.
  • Prismatic Spray: Rays hit subjects with variety of effects.
  • Restoration*: Restores drained level and attribute reducing effects.
  • Sequester: Subject is invisible to sight and scrying.
  • Shadow Walk: Step into shadow to travel rapidly.
  • Simulacrium: Creates clone, but weaker and without all memory.
  • Telepathic Bond: Link lets allies communicate.
  • Vision: Quickly reveals legends about person, place, or thing.

8th-Level Illusionist Spells

  • Antipathy*: Object or location attracts/repels certain creatures.
  • Distort Reality: Alters reality within spell limits.
  • Find the Path*: Shows most direct way to a location.
  • Incendiary Cloud: Smoke deals 4d6 damage/rd. and limits vision.
  • Mass Charm: Multiple creatures believe they are caster’s ally.
  • Polymorph: Gives caster or one subject a new form.
  • Power Word Blind: Creatures with up to 100 hit points are blinded.
  • Prismatic Wall: Wall’s colors have array of effects.
  • Screen: Illusion hides area from vision, scrying.
  • Sunburst: Blinds within 10 ft., deals 3d6 damage, harms undead.
  • Trap the Soul: Imprisons subject within gem.
  • Wind Walk: The caster and the caster’s allies turn vaporous and travel fast.

9th-Level Illusionist Spells

  • Clone: Shows most direct way to a location.

  • Heal*: Multiple creatures believe they are caster’s ally.

  • Polymorph Any Object: Creatures with up to 100 hit points are blinded.
