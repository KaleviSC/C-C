Tiefling

Languages: Common, Infernal, Abyssal, Draconic, Dwarven, Elven, Gnome, Goblin, Halfling, Infernal, and Orc.

Size: Medium

Movement: 30 feet

Typical Classes: Fighters, rangers, wizards, clerics, assassins, bards

Attribute Modifiers: +1 Intelligence, +1 Charisma, -1 Strength, -1 Wisdom

Rogue and Assassin Modifier: +2 move silently, +2 hide, +1 find traps

ELEMENTAL RESISTANCE

Tieflings suffer only half damage against fire, water earth, air, cold and lightning attacks.

DARKVISION

Tieflings can see in complete darkness for up to 60 feet. Darkvision produces images that are in shades of gray, but it is otherwise like normal sight. Tieflings can function well with no light at all. Bright lights, such as from a lantern or other light source, spoil darkvision. A gnome requires one minute to adjust his or her eyes when a light source is extinguished before gaining full use of darkvision.

CREATE DARKNESS

Tieflings can create magical darkness for 10 minutes (20 foot radius) similiar to the spell darkness once per day.
