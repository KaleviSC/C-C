Wizard Spell List

Wizard Spells

0th-Level Wizard Spells (Cantrips)

  • Arcane Mark: Inscribes a personal rune (visible or invisible).
  • Dancing Lights: Creates figment torches or other lights.
  • Detect Magic: Detects magical aura and its strength along 50 ft. path.
  • Detect Poison: Detects poison in one creature or small object.
  • Endure Elements Protection from natural elements and weather.
  • Ghost Sound Figment sounds.
  • Light Object shines like a torch.
  • Mage Hand: Five-pound telekinesis.
  • Mending: Makes minor repairs on an object.
  • Message: Short, whispered communication at a distance.
  • Open/Close Opens or closes small or light things.
  • Prestidigitation: Performs minor tricks or tasks.

1st-Level Wizard Spells

  • Alter Size*: Object or creature grows or shrinks 10%/level (max 50%).
  • Burning Hands: 1d2 +1/level fire damage.
  • Change Self: Changes the caster’s appearance.
  • Charm Person: Makes one person the caster’s friend.
  • Comprehend Languages: Understands spoken and written languages.
  • Erase: Mundane or magical writing vanishes.
  • Feather Fall: Objects or creatures fall slowly.
  • Floating Disk: 3 ft. diameter horizontal disk that holds 1000 lbs.
  • Hold Portal: Holds door shut.
  • Identify: Determines single feature of magic item.
  • Jump: Make astounding leaps 1 min/level.
  • Magic Missile: Strikes for 1d4+1 damage; +1 missile/two levels.
  • Protection from Alignment*: +2 AC and saves, plus other defenses.
  • Read Magic: Read magical writings, scrolls, and spellbooks.
  • Shield: Invisible disc raises AC and blocks magic missiles.
  • Shocking Grasp: Electric touch deals 1d8 +1/level damage.
  • Sleep: Put 2d4 HD of creatures into comatose slumber.
  • Spider Climb: Grants ability to walk on walls and ceilings.
  • Summon Familiar: Summons & binds unique companion to wizard.
  • Unseen Servant: Creates invisible force that obeys the caster’s commands.

2nd-Level Wizard Spells

  • Acid Arrow: Make ranged attack to deal 2d4/ two lvls acid damage.
  • Continual Flame: Makes a permanent, heatless flame.
  • Darkness*: Creates 20 ft. rad. supernatural darkness.
  • Detect Thoughts*: Allows “listening” to surface thoughts.
  • Enhance Attribute*: Raises attribute score for 1 hr/level.
  • Fog Cloud: Fog obscures vision.
  • Invisibility: Subject is invisible until it attacks.
  • Knock*: Magically opens door, item, chest, etc.
  • Levitate: Subject moves up and down at the caster’s direction.
  • Locate Object*: Senses direction toward object (specific or type).
  • Magic Mouth: Speaks once when triggered.
  • Mirror Image: Creates 1d4+1 decoy duplicates of the caster.
  • Protection From Arrows: Subject immune to most ranged attacks.
  • Pyrotechnics: Turns fire into blinding light or choking smoke.
  • Ray of Enfeeblement: -1 to hit and -1 to damage rolls for every four levels of the caster.
  • Rope Trick: Up to eight creatures hide in extradimensional space.
  • Scare: Panics one creature up to 5 HD.
  • See Invisibility: Reveals invisible creatures or objects.
  • Shatter: Sonic vibration damages objects or crystalline creatures.
  • Web: Area fills with sticky webs, trap creatures in 20 ft. X 20 ft. area.

3rd-Level Wizard Spells

  • Blink: Makes harder to hit and other effects.
  • Clairaudience/Clairvoyance: Hear or see at a distance for 1 min./level.
  • Dispel Magic: Cancels magical spells and effects.
  • Explosive Runes: Deals 6d6 damage when read.
  • Fireball: 1d6 damage/level in 40 ft. diameter sphere.
  • Fly: Subject flies at speed of 90.
  • Gaseous Form: Subject becomes insubstantial and can fly slowly.
  • Gust of Wind: Blows away or knocks down smaller creatures.
  • Haste*: Doubles move and number of attacks.
  • Hold Person: Holds one person helpless; 1 round/level.
  • Invisibility Sphere: Makes everyone within 10 ft. invisible.
  • Lightning Bolt: Electricity deals 1d6 damage/ level.
  • Magic Circle (against Chaos, Evil, Good, Law): As protection spell, but 10 ft. radius.
  • Nondetection: Hides subject from divination, scrying.
  • Stinking Cloud: Nauseating vapors that make helpless & limit sight.
  • Suggestion: Compels subject to follow stated course of action.
  • Summon Lesser Monster: Calls 3 HD or less monsters to aid caster.
  • Tiny Hut: Creates shelter for 10 creatures.
  • Tongues*: Speak any language.
  • Water Breathing: Subjects can breathe underwater.

4th-Level Wizard Spells

  • Arcane Eye: Caster can see with invisible, moving eye.
  • Charm Monster: Makes monster believe it is the caster’s ally.
  • Confusion: Makes subjects behave randomly for 1 rd/level.
  • Detect Scrying: Alerts caster of magical eavesdropping.
  • Dimension Door: Teleports caster (and 500 lbs.) up to 450 ft.
  • Fear: Subjects flee for 1 round/level.
  • Fire Shield*: Attackers take damage; protects against cold attacks.
  • Fire Trap: Opened object deals 1d4 +1/level damage.
  • Hallucinatory Terrain: Makes type of terrain appear like another.
  • Ice Storm: Hail deals 5d6 damage in cylinder 50 ft. across and tall.
  • Locate Creature: Indicates direction to individual/type of creature.
  • Minor Globe Of Invulnerability: Stops 1st to 3rd-level spell effects.
  • Mnemonic Enhancer: Caster prepares or retains additional spells.
  • Polymorph: Gives caster or one subject a new form.
  • Remove Curse*: Frees person or object from curses.
  • Resilient Sphere: Force globe protects but traps one subject.
  • Scrying: Spies on subject from a distance.
  • Shout: Deafens all within cone and deals 2d6 damage.
  • Wall Of Fire: Fiery curtain or ring deals damage to those near or passing through
  • Wall Of Ice: Creates very hard sheet or hemisphere of ice.

5th-Level Wizard Spells

  • Animate Dead*: Creates and controls undead skeletons and zombies.
  • Blind Elemental: Traps elemental to perform task.
  • Cloudkill: Poison fog 3 HD or less; 4-6 HD save or die.
  • Cone Of Cold: 1d6 cold damage/level.
  • Contact Other Plane: Ask questions of extraplanar entity.
  • Faithful Hound: Phantom dog can guard, attack.
  • Feeblemind: Subject’s Int drops to below an animal’s.
  • Hold Monster: As hold person, but any creature.
  • Magic Jar: Enables possession of another creature.
  • Passwall: Creates temporary passages through walls.
  • Permanency: Makes certain spells permanent with a cost.
  • Secret Chest: Hides magic chest for caster to retrieve when needed.
  • Summon Monster: Calls 6 HD or less monsters to aid caster.
  • Telekinesis: Lifts or moves 25 lb./level at long range.
  • Telepathic Bond: Link lets allies communicate.
  • Teleport: Instantly transports the caster anywhere.
  • Transmute Mud And Rock: Change mud into rock, or vice-versa.
  • Wall Of Force: Wall, sphere, or hemisphere is immune to damage.
  • Wall Of Iron: Creates vertical sheet of tough iron.
  • Wall Of Stone: Creates a stone wall that can be shaped.

6th-Level Wizard Spells

  • Anti-Magic Shell: Creates mobile sphere that negates magic.
  • Chain Lightning: Deals 1d6 damage/level; strikes multiple targets.
  • Control Weather: Changes weather in local area.
  • Disintegrate: Destroys one creature or object.
  • Geas: Forces creature to fulfil a quest.
  • Globe Of Invulnerability: Stops 1st to 4th level spell effects.
  • Guards And Wards: Array of magic effects protect area.
  • Legend Lore: Learn tales about a person, place, or thing.
  • Mass Suggestion: Compels one subject/level to follow course of action.
  • Move Earth: Digs trenches and build hills.
  • Project Earth: Illusory double can talk and cast spells.
  • Transmute Flesh And Stone: Turns subject creature into statue, or restores petrified creature.

7th-Level Wizard Spells

  • Delayed Blast Fireball: 1d6 +1/ level fire damage, can delay for 5 rds.
  • Finger Of Death: Kills one subject.
  • Greater Scrying: As scrying, but faster and longer.
  • Instant Summons: Prepared object appears in the caster’s hand.
  • Limited Wish: Alters reality within spell limits.
  • Mass Invisibility: As invisibility, but affects all in range.
  • Phase Door: Invisible passage through wood or stone.
  • Power Word Stun: Creatures with up to 120 hp are stunned.
  • Sequester: Subject is invisible to sight and scrying.
  • Summon Greater Monster: Calls 9 HD or less monsters to aid caster.
  • Teleport Without Error: As teleport, but no off-target arrival.
  • Vanish: As teleport, but affects a touched object.

8th-Level Wizard Spells

  • Antipathy*: Object or location attracts/repels certain creatures.
  • Binding: Array of techniques to imprison a creature.
  • Clone: Duplicates a creature.
  • Incendiary Cloud: Smoke deals 4d6 damage/rd. and limits vision.
  • Mass Charm: Multiple creatures believe they are caster’s ally.
  • Maze: Traps subject in extradimensional maze.
  • Mind Blank: Subject is immune to mental magic and scrying.
  • Polymorph Any Object: Changes any creature or object into anything else.
  • Power Word Blind: Creatures with up to 100 hit points are blinded.
  • Symbol: Triggered runes have array of effects.
  • Teleport Circle: All creatures inside circle teleport to designated spot.
  • Trap The Soul: Imprisons subject within gem.

9th-Level Wizard Spells

  • Astral Projection: Projects the caster & companions into astral plane.
  • Disjunction: Dispels magic, disenchants magic items.
  • Gate: Connects two planes for travel or summoning.
  • Imprisonment*: Entombs subject beneath the earth.
  • Meteor Swarm: Multiple fireballs deal damage in path.
  • Power Word Kill: One tough subject or many weak ones die.
  • Prismatic Sphere: Multi-colored protective sphere.
  • Refuge: Alters item to teleport its possessor to the caster.
  • Shapechange: Transforms caster into any creature once per round.
  • Temporal Stasis: Puts subject into suspended animation.
  • Time Stop: The caster stops time and acts freely for 1d4+1 rounds.
  • Wish: Alters reality as desired, but with risk.
